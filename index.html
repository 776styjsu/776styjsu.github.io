About

My research aims to make software that interacts with the physical world verifiable and reliable. I use a mix of static and dynamic analysis, fuzzing, and spec inference to test and verify VLA systems and autonomy stacks.

  • Interests: Program analysis, testing, mutation analysis, verification for AI/robotics
  • Email: you@example.edu
  • Office: Rice Hall, UVA
  • Links: Google Scholar · GitHub · LinkedIn

Research

Broadly, I explore: (1) generating and checking task-specific specifications for AI-enabled systems, and (2) scaling feedback-directed random testing with learned heuristics and purity-aware generation.
