Research

My research aims to make software that interacts with the physical world verifiable and reliable.

Broadly, I explore:

  1. Testing and verification methods for CPS safety and robustness.
  2. Applying and extending program analysis techniques to CPS and AI/ML-enabled systems.
  3. Automated testing, test oracles, and metrics for correctness, performance, and safety.
  4. Bridging the gap between simulation and real-world deployment in robotics.
